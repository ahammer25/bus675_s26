# Sir Quacks-a-Lot: Central Park Rescue (Dad Mode Activated)


## Story
You play as Sir Quacks-a-Lot, a proud father duck who was left in charge of watching his three ducklings while his wife, Quackie O, takes a well-deserved vacation. Everything was going fine… until you looked away for literally one second and Pip, Mip, and Sip disappeared somewhere in Central Park.
Now Sir Quacks-a-Lot has to explore the park, deal with chaotic enemies like raccoons, off-leash dogs, and overly excited kids, and search different locations to find his ducklings before Quackie O gets home.
No pressure… but if she comes back and the ducklings are still missing, you’re in serious trouble.

## How to Play

### Running the Game
```bash
python game.py
```

### Commands
You control Sir Quacks-a-Lot by typing commands in the terminal. Here are the main ones:
go [direction] → Move somewhere (north, south, east, west)
look → Re-describe the current location
fight or attack → Fight an enemy in the area
take [item] → Pick up an item you see
use [item] → Use an item from your inventory
talk [person] → Talk to someone nearby (like the old man in the park)
inventory or i → Check what you're carrying
help → Show the list of commands
quit → Exit the game
During combat, you’ll be asked to type:
attack → Attack the enemy
run → Try to escape (50% chance)

## Goal
[What does the player need to do to win?]
Your goal is to find all three missing ducklings (Pip, Mip, and Sip) somewhere around Central Park before the timer runs out and Quackie O comes home.
If you collect all three ducklings in your inventory, you win the game.
If you run out of turns or Sir Quacks-a-Lot gets defeated in combat, the game ends.

## Tips
[Any hints for players?]
Explore every location carefully, the ducklings could be anywhere.
Some enemies are stronger than others, so sometimes it’s smarter to run.
Pick up items you find because some of them can heal you or boost your strength.
If you see the Old Man in the park, talk to him — he might give you something helpful.
Keep an eye on the timer (number of rounds left)… Quackie O is coming home soon.
